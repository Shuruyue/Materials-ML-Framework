Epochal Paper Atlas

How breakthrough papers change technology

This atlas studies landmark engineering and STEM papers by the progress mechanisms they introduced, not by discipline labels alone.

Core thesis

Epochal papers usually do one or more irreversible things: reveal a hidden variable, create a new control surface, make search tractable, create infrastructure that scales, or turn a scientific possibility into an engineering platform.

This release

The current atlas targets 1000+ high-quality papers from roughly the last 30 years, while leaving room for older precursors when they still structure the technology landscape today.
